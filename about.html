About BACS

The Boston Area Constable Service is veteran owned, and run by two Boston Special Police Officers. Constable Do and Constable Singh have patrolled together in both Roxbury and South Boston, detecting, deterring, and reacting to crimes. Originally friends during the Special Police Officer academy, both Do and Singh were assigned together near the housing developments of Roxbury. They shared their first arrest together, where they both arrested a male who was violent towards another, and assaulted them with a dangerous weapon. Both constables of the Boston Area Constable Service are trained in both Criminal and Civil law of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

Our Team

Constable Do
Job Title

Constable Do is a US Army soldier and a Boston Special Police Officer. Both him and Constable Singh have patrolled the housing projects in the City of Boston. Constable Do speaks Vietnamese, Spanish and German.

Constable Singh
Job Title

Constable Singh is a current Boston Special Police Officer who still patrols the area of Roxbury and Dorchester's housing projects. Both him and Constable Do have worked together in the City of Boston. Constable Singh speaks both Hindi and Punjab.

Deputy Constable Pinto
Job Title

Deputy Constable Pinto is a father, a husband, and a person who is very knowledgeable in firearms. Though not a Special Police Officer, Deputy Constable Pinto is trained on Criminal Law in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. Deputy Constable Pinto speaks Spanish.

Deputy Constable Sentalimov
Job Title
